> 01 / THESIS

Why this data,
and why now.

3 POINTS / 220 WORDS
> 01 / CONTEXT

A new asset class.
No infrastructure.

Kalshi is CFTC regulated. Polymarket is back in the US. Volume crossed $10B in 2025. Every major bank has a prediction market desk under research now. There is still no Bloomberg. No Refinitiv. No Koyfin. Tick data vendors do not exist.

> 02 / MOAT

History is a moat
that compounds.

A competitor who starts capturing in 2027 cannot retroactively capture what we are capturing today. Every day the archive runs, it becomes harder to replicate. This is not a feature that can be cloned. It is a position in time.

> 03 / DEPTH

Prices alone
are not enough.

Backtesting strategies on event markets requires knowing what the world looked like at each timestamp. News. Reddit. Polls. Weather. Game scores. Stele archives the surrounding world in sync with every market tick. Nobody else does this.

> 02 / COVERAGE

Two data layers.
One timestamp.

CAPTURE ACTIVE / 1s CADENCE

MARKET LAYER LIVE

> Venues
Kalshi, Polymarket. Manifold and Insight on the roadmap.
> Fields
Mid, bid/ask, L2 order book depth, trade prints, volume, open interest, resolution outcome.
> Cadence
1 second for liquid contracts. 60 seconds for the long tail. All contracts, no gaps.
> Coverage
Economics, politics, climate, crypto, sports, culture, science. Every active contract captured.
> Format
Parquet, CSV, JSONL. SQL query interface in beta.

CONTEXT LAYER NEW

> News
Synchronized headline snapshots from Bloomberg, Reuters, AP, and topic-specific feeds at every tick.
> Social
Reddit subreddit state, top comments, velocity. X/Twitter posts for flagged keywords.
> Polling
538, Nate Silver, YouGov, Morning Consult, plus state-level trackers for electoral markets.
> On-chain
ETH/BTC mempool, DEX volumes, wallet flows for crypto-adjacent contracts.
> Telemetry
Weather (NOAA), sports live scores, seismic (USGS), game state for esports markets.

> 03 / TECHNICAL

The specifics

5 QUESTIONS
Q.01

How much data, today?

2.4B market ticks across Kalshi and Polymarket since Q3 2024. 18TB of headline and social context, keyed to market timestamps. Growing at roughly 120GB per week.

SCALE
Q.02

Latency of capture.

Liquid contracts captured at 1 second cadence. Wire-to-disk latency under 400ms for Kalshi, under 900ms for Polymarket. Context layer sampled at 15 seconds to 5 minutes depending on source.

PERF
Q.03

Can I get redistributable data?

Redistribution rights being cleared with Kalshi and Polymarket. Until then, access is research and internal-use only, under a standard data license. Public commercial API follows once rights are in place.

LEGAL
Q.04

Pricing.

Academic research: free. Individual traders: free tier plus metered query-volume pricing. Funds and institutions: custom, based on feed breadth and refresh rate. Quoted on request.

PRICE

Why not scrape this myself?

Because you would be starting now. We started earlier. A dataset that began eighteen months ago cannot be built by starting today, no matter how good your engineers are. The archive is a position in time, not a crawler.
